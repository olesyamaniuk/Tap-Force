About the Game

Step back into the '90s arcade era with Tap Force, a retro pixel RPG where auto battles decide the fate of Metro City! Assemble a team of 16-bit fighters, expand your weapon shop business, and take down enemies, bosses, and even real players from around the world. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive warrior, Tap Force delivers the perfect mix of action and strategy.

  • Classic 16-bit pixel art style with modern gameplay.

  • Recruit & train fighters to build your ultimate team.

  • Expand your weapon shop and earn money even when offline.

  • Battle enemies & PvP players worldwide.

How to Play

  • Recruit Fighters

    Unlock and train 16-bit pixel warriors to form your ultimate team.

  • Run a Weapon Shop

    Sell powerful gear and earn gold even while AFK!

  • Auto Battle Combat

    Watch your team take down enemies in intense battles.

  • Defeat Bosses & PvP Opponents

    Climb the global ranks and dominate the streets of Metro City!

Game Features

  • Retro 16-bit Graphics – A nostalgic arcade-style world brought to life.

  • Easy Auto Battles – No complex controls, just strategy & upgrades!

  • Epic Fighters to Collect – Recruit unique pixel warriors with powerful abilities.

  • Idle Weapon Shop System – Earn money even while you're away!

  • PvP & Global Leaderboards – Battle against players from all over the world.

  • Daily Rewards & Events – Unlock exclusive items and characters.

Game Atmosphere

  • Immerse yourself in Metro City, a crime-filled pixel world where only the toughest fighters can survive.
  • With nostalgic arcade vibes, neon-lit cityscapes, and pixel-perfect action, Tap Force delivers an experience that blends classic gaming with modern mobile RPG mechanics.

FAQ

  • Is Tap Force free to play?

    Yes! The game is free to download and play, with optional in-game purchases.

  • Can I play offline?

    You can earn rewards offline through the weapon shop, but battles require an internet connection.

  • What devices is the game available on?

    Tap Force is available on both Android and iOS.

  • Will there be new fighters and updates?

    Yes! New characters, weapons, and events are added regularly.

  • Is there PvP?

    Absolutely! Compete against other players in intense online battles.
